# Welcome to NotePo

NotePo is an elegant and secure note-taking application designed to help you capture your thoughts effortlessly.

- **Cloud Sync:** Keep your notes synced securely across all your devices.
- **End-to-End Encryption:** Your privacy is our priority. Protect your notes with a passkey only you know.
- **Customizable:** Choose from beautiful themes, font sizes, and premium app icons.
